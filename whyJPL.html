Lets get in to some examples and what I believe to be the right strategies to employ for serious players in order to start winning at cornhole. Remember: these winning cornhole strategies could be exactly what you need to elevate your game to the Next level. Example 1) Red threw first and laid up the perfect block. As a beginner player your number one option is to double block ( throwing your bag right in front of the red one). It causes the player you are throwing against to make a decision. Is he/she content throwing for a wash or does he/she want to start throwing airmails? The risks are low for the double block. If you miss left or right, it is still 1-1. If the other player goes for an airmail and misses, you gave yourself a point. The best airmailers in the world are around 70% accurate. So even if you are playing elite talent, you still have a 30% chance of making a point. Most average players are under 50%.
